Onion Dip

Description

This is a recipe that I got from my mother-in-law. Its my fiances and mine's favorite onion dip, and I get compliments on it everytime I have made it for anyone else. It's super easy to make and only takes two ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Jumbo Yellow Onions
  • Cream Cheese

Steps

  1. Rough and finely dice onions
  2. Mix in with cream cheese
  3. Bake for some time in some heat
