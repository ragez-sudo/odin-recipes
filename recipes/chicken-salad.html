Chicken Salad

Description

The chicken salad recipe is a favorite of my fiance and me. It is realtively simple and uses few ingredients, most of which you would probably already have on hand.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Chicken broth or bouillon
  • Celery
  • Shallots
  • Green Onion
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Herbs - Sage, Thyme, Dill
  • Celery Salt
  • Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Add chicken, sage, and thyme to broth or bouillon.
  2. Start broth to a boil. Once boiling, cover with a lid and reduce to a simmer. Let cook for 15-30 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  3. While chicken is cooking, prep raw ingredients.
    1. Thinly slice shallots into rings
    2. Dice celery
    3. Chop up green onions
  4. After chicken is cooked and cooled, remove bone and skin.
  5. Shred chicken - this can more easily be done with a kitchenaid dough mixer attachment on low setting. Just be sure to not overmix or it can make the shredded chicken more tough.
  6. Add mayonnaise and dijon in about a 10:1 ratio to the chicken, celery, shallots, and green onions, and dill. Mix well and add celery salt and black pepper to taste.
