Pulled Pork

Description

Pulled pork recipe using a dutch oven.

Ingredients

  • Pork butt or shoulder
  • Large white or yellow onions
  • Diced Garlic
  • Cooking Oil
  • Cooking wine
  • Beef Broth
  • Onion Soup Powder
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Rub

Instructions

  1. Head up dutch oven on stove with oil. Sear all sides of butt or shoulder.
  2. Once seared remove from dutch oven and apply rub generously.
  3. Add onions cut into quarters. Once they get a little bit of color add garlic.
  4. Once garlic is fragrant add white wine and vinegar to deglaze. Cook both down until less sharp of a bite.
  5. Add meat back to dutch oven and pour in broth. It's okay if not all is covered.
  6. Cover with lid and add to 300 degree oven for 6-7 hours, or until meat is tender and falls apart.
  7. Pro tip: Use immersion blender to emulsify whatever onions are left after pulling meat out of remaining liquid, slowly boil down and add a slurry to turn into gravy.
